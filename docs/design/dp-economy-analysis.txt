HEROES OF LEGEND — DP ECONOMY ANALYSIS
Character Creation: Level 0 → Level 1
============================================================

SYSTEM RULES (confirmed)
-----------------------
- 10 levels of play. Adept abilities unlock at Level 3, Master at Level 7.
- Character development starts at Level 0. Play starts at Level 1.
- Disciplines: Prerequisites. Purchased for 1 DP each.
- Skills: Rolled with 3d6. Novice +1 (1 DP), Adept +2 (2 DP), Master +3 (3 DP).
- Abilities: Special powers/spells. Novice 1 DP, Adept 2 DP, Master 4 DP.
- Levels 2+: 8 DP each.
- HP: 10 + Fortitude + Knowledge at Level 1. +Fortitude per level (min 1).
- Attributes: 3 points to distribute, range -2 to +2.
- All characters start with 3 General Disciplines.
- Class, Ancestry, and Background each grant additional Disciplines.

CHARACTER CREATION: LEVEL 0 → LEVEL 1
-------------------------------------
Level 0 — Ancestry & Background ("Early Life"):
  DP = 8 + Knowledge modifier + Fortitude modifier.
  Example: Knowledge +2, Fortitude +1 → 8 + 2 + 1 = 11 DP.
  Example: Knowledge -1, Fortitude -2 → 8 - 1 - 2 = 5 DP.

  In this phase you buy skills, abilities, and Disciplines that reflect your
  early life — ancestry culture, background training, formative experiences.
  You CANNOT bank DP. Use it or lose it. No Adept or Master purchases
  (those gates haven't opened yet).

Level 1 — Choose Your Class:
  DP = 8 (flat). You've chosen your path — now train in it.
  Buy class-specific skills, abilities, and Disciplines.
  Unspent DP CAN be banked to Level 2. This is when PLAY begins.

After Level 0 + Level 1, your character is complete. Levels 2+ get 8 DP each.

Total creation DP = Level 0 (8+K+F) + Level 1 (8) = 16 + Knowledge + Fortitude.

KEY INSIGHTS
------------
1. Knowledge modifier directly affects Phase 1 DP. High Knowledge characters
   (wizards) get 9-10 DP in Level 0 vs 7-8 for low Knowledge (fighters).
   This creates organic differentiation — smart characters learn more
   before specializing.

2. Phase 1 "use or lose" forces breadth. Every character ends up with
   6-8 skills at Novice or better by Level 5 — not just combat stats.
   Borin knows dwarven lore and smithing. Kara knows survival. Lyra knows
   the underworld. These aren't "wasted" DP — they're character depth
   that the system mandates.

3. Wizards benefit most from high Knowledge. Thalia loaded up on Fire(3)
   and Energy(2) Disciplines in Level 0 — completely skipping prerequisite
   buys that would have cost DP later. This is the wizard's reward for
   investing in Knowledge.

4. Low Fortitude penalizes Phase 1 DP. Vex and Pip (Fort -1) only get
   7-9 DP vs Borin's 9. This is the cost of being fragile — you spent
   your youth studying or performing, not building endurance.

5. Phase 2 DP can be banked. Most characters bank 3-6 DP from Level 1,
   giving them a head start on Level 2 Adept upgrades.

6. Budget 8 DP/level works. No character is starved. The spread is 46-58%
   spent by L5, with all characters having their core identity online
   and 20-26 DP banked for future progression.


RECOMMENDATION
--------------
Two-phase creation confirmed at 8 DP/level:
  Phase 1 (Ancestry/Background): 8 + Knowledge + Fortitude DP. Use or lose.
  Phase 2 (Class): 8 DP. Unspent banks to Level 2.
  Levels 2+: 8 DP each.
  Adept at L3, Master at L7.

This creates meaningful differentiation between high-Knowledge (wizard)
and high-Fortitude (fighter) characters while forcing background depth
through the Phase 1 "use or lose" mechanic.
