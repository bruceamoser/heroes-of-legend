HEROES OF LEGEND — DP ECONOMY ANALYSIS
Use-or-Lose at Every Level
============================================================

CORE RULE: DP is use-or-lose at EVERY level. No banking. Spend it or lose it.
This forces breadth at low levels, then deepening via Adept (L3) and Master (L7).

SYSTEM RULES
------------
- 10 levels. Adept at L3, Master at L7. Play starts at L1.
- Level 0 (Ancestry/Background): DP = 8 + Knowledge + Fortitude. Use or lose.
- Level 1 (Class): 8 DP. Use or lose.
- Levels 2+: 8 DP each. Use or lose.
- Skills: Novice +1 (1 DP), Adept +2 (2 DP), Master +3 (3 DP).
- Abilities: Novice 1 DP, Adept 2 DP, Master 4 DP.
- Disciplines: 1 DP each. Prerequisites for skills/abilities.
- HP: 10 + Fortitude + Knowledge at L1. +Fortitude/level (min 1).
- 3 General Disciplines at creation. Class/Ancestry/Background grant more.

RESULT: Characters are broadly competent by level 5 — 8-11 skills at
Adept or better, 5-8 abilities. Wizards have more Disciplines (10-12)
because they buy them to unlock spell chains. Everyone has their core
combat/class identity online by L3, then layers on depth at L4-L5.

The Adept gate at L3 creates a natural rhythm: levels 0-2 build breadth
(Novice everything), levels 3-5 deepen (Adept upgrades). Master at L7
will be the next big power spike.

KEY INSIGHT
-----------
Use-or-lose eliminates "analysis paralysis" — players don't agonize over
saving DP for later. Every level is a spending puzzle: "What am I doing
with my 8 DP right now?" This keeps character progression engaging at
every level rather than front-loading or hoarding.


RECOMMENDATION
--------------
Confirmed: use-or-lose at every level. 8 DP/level (L0: 8+Knowledge+Fortitude).
No banking. Adept at L3, Master at L7. Characters build broad, then deep.
