= Introduction

*Heroes of Legend* is a fantasy tabletop roleplaying game. You and your friends create heroes, embark on adventures, and tell epic stories together. One player — the Dungeon Architect — presents challenges. Everyone else controls a hero and decides how to respond.

== What You Need
- This book
- Three six-sided dice (3d6) per player
- A character sheet (pencil and paper or digital)
- Friends and imagination

== Core Mechanic
Roll *3d6 + Attribute + Skill + Modifiers*. Results of 1-6 are Weak, 7-12 are Standard, and 13-18+ are Strong. Three natural 6s is a Critical. Three natural 1s is a Fumble.

== How to Use This Book
- *New players:* Start with chapter 2 — character creation
- *DAs:* Chapters 3-8 cover core mechanics. Chapter 19 is your guide
- *Reference:* Chapters 11-12 (spells), 15-17 (equipment), 20 (monsters)

== Example of Play
*DA:* "The goblin chieftain raises his rusted axe. What do you do?"

*Kael:* "I draw my longsword and strike!"

*DA:* "Roll 3d6."

*Kael:* rolls "4, 3, 5 — that's 12, plus my Brawn +2 and Longsword skill +2 makes 16! Strong hit!"

*DA:* "Roll Strong damage."

*Kael:* "3d8+2... 15 damage!"

*DA:* "Your blade cleaves through the chieftain's leather armor. He staggers, bleeding. The remaining goblins hesitate — their leader is broken."
