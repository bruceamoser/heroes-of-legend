= Combat

Combat in Heroes of Legend is fast and cinematic. Every swing matters.

== Initiative
At combat start, each participant rolls 1d6 + Agility modifier. Highest goes first. Ties: higher Agility wins, then roll-off.

== The Combat Round
On your turn, take one *Standard Action* and one *Move Action*, plus any number of *Free Actions* and one *Reaction* per round.

[%header,cols="2,4"]
|===
| Action | Examples
| *Standard* | Attack, cast spell, use ability, dash, help, hide
| *Move* | Move up to speed, stand up, mount/dismount
| *Bonus* | Class features, quick items (conditional)
| *Reaction* | Opportunity attack, shield block (1/round)
| *Free* | Talk, drop item, gesture (unlimited)
|===

== Making an Attack
Attacks always hit. Roll 3d6 + modifiers to determine damage tier:
- *Weak:* Roll weapon's Weak damage (glancing blow)
- *Standard:* Roll weapon's Standard damage (solid hit)
- *Strong:* Roll weapon's Strong damage (devastating strike)
- *Critical:* Maximize Strong damage + critical bonus

== Damage Types
*Physical:* Slashing, Piercing, Bludgeoning
*Elemental:* Fire, Cold, Lightning, Acid, Poison
*Magical:* Force, Radiant, Necrotic, Psychic

== Conditions
[%header,cols="2,4,3"]
|===
| Condition | Effect | Ends
| *Blinded* | Attacks reduced one tier | Source duration
| *Charmed* | Cannot attack charmer | Save ends
| *Deafened* | Perception disadvantage | Source duration
| *Frightened* | Cannot approach source | Save ends
| *Grappled* | Speed 0 | Escape action
| *Incapacitated* | No actions | Source duration
| *Invisible* | Cannot be targeted directly | Attack/action ends
| *Paralyzed* | Incapacitated + auto-crit if hit | Save ends
| *Poisoned* | Disadvantage on attacks | Save ends
| *Prone* | Melee adv vs you, ranged disadv | Stand up (move)
| *Restrained* | Speed 0, attack disadv | Escape action
| *Stunned* | Incapacitated + cannot move | Save ends
| *Unconscious* | Incapacitated + prone + unaware | Healing or save
|===

== Dying
At 0 HP, you fall unconscious. Each round, roll 3d6:
- *Strong (13+):* Stabilize at 1 HP
- *Standard (7-12):* Remain unconscious, stable
- *Weak (1-6):* Take 1 wound, continue dying
- *Fumble (3×1):* Death
- *Critical (3×6):* Regain consciousness at half HP

== Cover
*Half cover:* -1 to attacker's roll. *Three-quarters cover:* -3. *Full cover:* Cannot be targeted.
