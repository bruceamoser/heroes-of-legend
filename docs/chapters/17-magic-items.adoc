[#chapter-magic-items]
= Magic Items

Magic items enhance heroes and may grant Disciplines.

[%header,cols="3,2,4"]
|===
| Item | Rarity | Effect
| *+1 Weapon* | Uncommon | +1 damage to all tiers
| *Flame Tongue* | Rare | +1d6 fire, requires 1 Fire die
| *Shield +1* | Uncommon | +1 DR
| *Cloak of Elvenkind* | Uncommon | Advantage on Stealth
| *Ring of Protection* | Rare | +1 DR, +1 to all saves
| *Staff of Power* | Very Rare | +2 to spell damage tiers
| *Deck of Many Things* | Legendary | Draw a card, fate unfolds
|===

Attunement: A character may attune to 3 magic items at a time. Artifacts do not count against this limit.

Discipline Items: Some items grant temporary dice while attuned. A *Fire Opal* grants 1 Fire die. A *Blade Master's Gauntlet* grants 1 Blade die. These count toward prerequisite requirements.
