= Armor & Shields

Armor reduces damage. Shields block actively.

[%header,cols="3,1,1,2"]
|===
| Armor | Dice | DR | Stealth
| Padded | — | -1 | —
| Leather | — | -2 | —
| Studded Leather | 1 Armor | -2 | —
| Chain Shirt | 1 Armor | -3 | Disadv
| Breastplate | 2 Armor | -4 | —
| Half Plate | 2 Armor | -4 | Disadv
| Chain Mail | 2 Armor | -5 | Disadv
| Plate | 3 Armor | -6 | Disadv
|===

== Shields
*Buckler:* 1 Protection, +1 DR. *Shield:* 1 Protection, +2 DR. *Tower Shield:* 2 Protection, +3 DR, half cover. Shield Block reaction: reduce one attack's damage tier by one step.

== Donning & Doffing
Light armor: 1 minute. Medium: 5 minutes. Heavy: 10 minutes. Shield: 1 action.
