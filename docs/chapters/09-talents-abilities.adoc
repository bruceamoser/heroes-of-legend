= Talents & Abilities

Talents are passive benefits. Abilities are active powers. Both are purchased with Development Points.

== DP Costs
[%header,cols="1,1,3"]
|===
| Tier | DP Cost | Description
| *Novice* | 1 DP | Basic competency
| *Adept* | 2 DP | Advanced mastery (requires Novice)
| *Master* | 4 DP | Legendary expertise (requires Adept)
|===

== General Talents
Available to all classes.

[%header,cols="2,1,4"]
|===
| Talent | Tier | Effect
| *Tough* | Novice | +2 max HP
| *Alert* | Novice | +2 initiative
| *Lucky* | Novice | Reroll one 1 per session
| *Skill Focus* | Novice | +1 to one skill (stacks with tier bonus)
| *Die Collector* | Adept | Gain one additional generic die
| *Iron Will* | Adept | +2 to all saves vs fear and charm
| *Combat Reflexes* | Adept | One extra reaction per round
| *Die Master* | Master | One specific die counts as 2 for prerequisites
|===

== Class Talents
Each class has 2-3 exclusive talents.

*Protector:* *Shield Wall* (Adept) -- adjacent allies gain +1 DR. *Unbreakable* (Master) -- once per session, ignore all damage from one attack.

*Blade:* *Weapon Focus* (Adept) -- +1 damage with chosen weapon category. *Death Blow* (Master) -- on Critical, deal maximum Strong damage + roll critical bonus twice.

*Arcanist:* *Spell Weaver* (Adept) -- maintain two concentration spells. *Arcane Reservoir* (Master) -- once per session, cast a Master spell twice.

*Shepherd:* *Blessed Touch* (Adept) -- healing spells always use Standard effect minimum. *Divine Intervention* (Master) -- once per session, prevent an ally's death.

== Ability Chains
Abilities (including spells) follow the same Novice → Adept → Master chain structure. See xref:chapters/10-magic-system.adoc[Magic System] for spell chains.
