= Glossary

*Adept:* Skill tier granting +2 bonus. Costs 2 DP.

*Always Hit:* Attacks do not roll to hit -- the 3d6 determines damage tier.

*Attribute:* One of six core stats: Brawn, Fortitude, Agility, Guile, Knowledge, Reason.

*Critical:* Three natural 6s on 3d6. Automatic Strong + bonus effect.

*DA:* Dungeon Architect -- the game master.

*DP:* Development Points -- spent to purchase skills and talents.

*DR:* Damage Reduction -- subtracted from incoming physical damage.

*Discipline:* Prerequisite resource representing mastery of a domain. E.g., Fire Discipline, Blade Discipline.

*Fumble:* Three natural 1s on 3d6. Automatic failure + complication.

*Generic Die:* A die that can substitute for any specific type at Novice tier.

*HP:* Health Points -- 10 + Brawn.

*Master:* Skill tier granting +3 bonus. Costs 4 DP.

*Novice:* Skill tier granting +1 bonus. Costs 1 DP.

*Spell Chain:* Three-tier spell progression -- Novice, Adept, Master.

*Standard:* 7-12 result on 3d6 -- full success.

*Strong:* 13-18+ result on 3d6 -- exceptional success.

*Talent:* Passive benefit purchased with DP.

*Weak:* 1-6 result on 3d6 -- partial success or complication.

*Wounded:* Below half HP -- healing is halved.
