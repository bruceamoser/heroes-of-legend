= Disciplines

The Discipline system is Heroes of Legend's signature mechanic. Characters don't just gain skills — they earn *Disciplines* representing their mastery of elements, weapons, and arcane forces.

== What Are Disciplines?

A Discipline represents a domain of expertise or power your hero has attuned to. They are prerequisites for skills, abilities, and talents. You don't roll these Disciplines — they're symbolic of your character's growing mastery.

*Example:* A warrior with 3 Blade Disciplines has spent years mastering swords. A mage with 3 Fire Disciplines has deep attunement to flame. A ranger with 2 Animal Disciplines has bonded with the wild.

[%header,cols="2,1,4"]
|===
| Category | Discipline | Represents
.5+| *Elemental* | *Fire* | Flame, heat, passion, destruction
| *Earth* | Stone, stability, endurance, crafting
| *Wind* | Air, speed, storms, precision
| *Water* | Ice, flow, healing, adaptation
.6+| *Weapon* | *Blades* | Swords, daggers — finesse and precision
| *Axes* | Axes, cleavers — brutal chopping power
| *Polearms* | Spears, halberds — reach and control
| *Archery* | Bows, crossbows — ranged precision
| *Heavy Weapon* | Greatswords, greataxes, mauls — two-handed power
| *Unarmed* | Fists, gauntlets, grappling — close-quarters combat
.2+| *Defense* | *Protection* | Shields, warding, guarding allies
| *Armor* | Heavy armor proficiency, damage soaking
.1+| *Primal* | *Animal* | Beasts, nature, shapeshifting, instinct
.1+| *Arcane* | *Energy* | Raw magical force, metamagic, spellcraft
|===

== Acquiring Disciplines

=== Starting Disciplines
All characters begin with *3 General Disciplines*. General Disciplines can be used as prerequisites for any basic skill or novice-level ability, but cannot meet requirements for Adept or Master tiers.

=== Class Dice
Your class grants specific Disciplines at creation:

[%header,cols="2,4"]
|===
| Class | Starting Disciplines
| Protector | 2 Armor, 2 Protection
| Blade | 3 Blades
| Arcanist | 2 Fire, 1 Energy, 1 Wind (or any 4 elemental)
| Shepherd | 2 Protection, 2 Animal
| Intellect | 3 of any type (flexible)
| Odd | 3 of any type, must be from different categories
| Leader | 2 Protection, 1 Energy, 1 any
| Unbalanced | 2 of one type, 2 of its opposite (Fire/Water or Earth/Wind)
|===

=== Progression
At levels 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, and 18, gain one additional Discipline of your choice. Class-favored dice (those your class grants at creation) are always available. Cross-class dice may require narrative justification or story events.

=== Magic Items
Rare magic items may grant temporary or permanent Disciplines while attuned or wielded.

== Disciplines as Prerequisites

Skills, abilities, and talents require specific Disciplines as prerequisites:

[%header,cols="3,2,1"]
|===
| Skill/Ability | Dice Required | Tier
| Longsword | 2 Blades | Adept
| Dagger | 1 Blade | Novice
| Greataxe | 2 Axes | Adept
| Fireball | 2 Fire + 1 Energy | Adept
| Volcanic Eruption | 3 Fire + 2 Earth + 1 Energy | Master
| Chain Mail | 2 Armor | Novice
| Shield Block | 1 Protection | Novice
| Animal Companion | 2 Animal | Adept
| Magic Missile | 1 Energy | Novice
| Counterspell | 2 Energy + 1 Reason | Adept
|===

=== General Disciplines Substitution
A Generic die can substitute for any specific type at the *Novice* tier only. For Adept or Master abilities, you must have the specific Disciplines listed.

== Worked Example

*Building a Spell Chain: Fire Magic*

Kael, an Arcanist, wants to master fire magic. At creation, he has 2 Fire dice and 1 Energy die from his class.

- *Novice (Firebolt):* Requires 1 Fire die. Kael qualifies immediately.
- *Adept (Fireball):* Requires 2 Fire + 1 Energy. Kael qualifies — he has exactly what's needed.
- *Master (Volcanic Eruption):* Requires 3 Fire + 2 Earth + 1 Energy. Kael needs 1 more Fire die and 2 Earth dice. He'll need to earn these through level progression or find a magic item.

At level 3, Kael gains his first Progression Discipline. He chooses Fire (now 3 Fire). At level 6, he gains another — Earth. At level 9, another Earth (now 2 Earth). At level 12, he finally has 3 Fire + 2 Earth + 1 Energy and can purchase Volcanic Eruption. This progression took 12 levels — Master-tier magic is earned, not given.
