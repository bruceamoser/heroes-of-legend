Properties: *Versatile* (two-handed for +1 die size), *Finesse* (add AG to damage), *Reach* (10 ft range), *Thrown* (range 20/60).

== Armor
Armor provides damage reduction (DR) — subtract from incoming physical damage.

[%header,cols="3,1,1"]
|===
| Armor | Dice Req | DR
| Padded | — | -1
| Leather | — | -2
| Studded Leather | 1 Armor | -2
| Chain Shirt | 1 Armor | -3
| Breastplate | 2 Armor | -4
| Chain Mail | 2 Armor | -5
| Plate | 3 Armor | -6
|===

Shields: *Buckler* (1 Protection, +1 DR), *Shield* (1 Protection, +2 DR), *Tower Shield* (2 Protection, +3 DR, provides half cover).

== Encumbrance
Slot-based system. You have 10 + (Brawn × 5) slots. Most items take 1 slot. Armor takes 2-4 slots. Two-handed weapons take 2 slots.
