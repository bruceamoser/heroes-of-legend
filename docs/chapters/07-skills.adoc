== Skill Cards

Each skill card shows your *X1/X2/X3* class cost tier, then the *Novice*,
*Adept*, and *Master* tiers. Adept and Master each unlock a maneuver --
powered by your Maneuver for the turn -- with a Discipline requirement.

DP costs per tier are shown in the <<Skill Tiers>> table at the top of this
chapter. X1 classes pay the lowest cost; X3 pay the highest.
