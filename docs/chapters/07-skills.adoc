== Skill Maneuvers

When you reach *Adept* or *Master* rank in a skill, you unlock a maneuver
that only that skill provides. Skill maneuvers are more powerful than basic
maneuvers -- they're why you invest in skills beyond Novice.

Each maneuver costs your Maneuver for the turn. Adept maneuvers require a
specific Discipline; Master maneuvers require a higher rank.
