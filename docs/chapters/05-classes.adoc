= Classes

Your class is your hero's calling — the role you play in the adventuring party.

== Class Overview

[%header,cols="2,3,3,3"]
|===
| Class | Role | Primary Dice | Signature
| *Protector* | Tank, defender | Armor, Protection | Damage reduction aura
| *Blade* | Melee striker | Blades, Axes, Polearms, Great Weapons | Bonus damage on Strong
| *Arcanist* | Arcane caster | Fire, Wind, Energy, Water, Earth | Cantrip mastery
| *Shepherd* | Divine caster | Protection, Animal, Energy | Bonus healing
| *Intellect* | Knowledge expert | (flexible, 3 of any) | Skill expertise
| *Odd* | Wildcard | (3 of any, different categories) | Random bonus
| *Leader* | Support | Protection, Energy | Ally buffs
| *Unbalanced* | High-risk | 2 opposing pairs | Corruption mechanic
|===

== The Protector
*"Stand behind me."*

Front-line defender. Gains 2 Armor and 2 Protection dice. Signature: *Bulwark* — allies within 10 ft reduce incoming damage by 1. Favored skills: Athletics, Intimidation, Melee Combat, Endurance, Resilience (1 DP each).

== The Blade
*"I am the weapon."*

Melee damage dealer. Gains 3 dice from Blades, Axes, Polearms, or Great Weapons. Signature: *Lethal Strike* — on a Strong hit, add +1d6 damage. Favored skills: Melee Combat, Acrobatics, Ranged Combat (1 DP each).

== The Arcanist
*"Fire answers to me."*

Arcane spellcaster. Gains 4 dice from Elemental or Energy types. Signature: *Cantrip Adept* — may use two cantrips per turn. Favored skills: Arcana, Alchemy (1 DP each).

== The Shepherd
*"The light guides my hand."*

Divine healer and protector. Gains 2 Protection, 2 Animal dice. Signature: *Healing Hands* — add +1d4 to all healing effects. Favored skills: Survival, Nature, Religion, Medicine (1 DP each).

== The Intellect
*"I've read about this."*

Knowledge and skill expert. Gains 3 dice of any type (flexible). Signature: *Expertise* — one skill treats its tier as one higher. Favored skills: History, Investigation (1 DP each).

== The Odd
*"I have a trick for that."*

Unpredictable wildcard. Gains 3 dice from different categories. Signature: *Wildcard* — once per session, roll on a d6 table for a random bonus. Favored skills: Stealth, Sleight of Hand, Streetwise (1 DP each).

== The Leader
*"Together, we are unstoppable."*

Party support and buffing. Gains 2 Protection, 1 Energy, 1 any. Signature: *Inspiring Word* — as an action, grant one ally +2 on their next roll. Favored skills: Persuasion, Resilience (1 DP each).

== The Unbalanced
*"Power has a price."*

High-risk, high-reward. Gains 2 dice from one type and 2 from its opposite (e.g., 2 Fire + 2 Water). Signature: *Edge of Chaos* — on a Strong hit, deal +2d6 damage but gain 1 Corruption. Favored skills: Melee Combat, Deception, Insight, Arcana (1 DP each).

== Progression

[%header,cols="1,4"]
|===
| Level | Gain
| 1 | Class signature, Starting Disciplines, 3 DP
| 2 | 2 DP
| 3 | Progression die, 2 DP
| 4 | Attribute increase, 2 DP
| 5 | Class feature upgrade, 3 DP
| 6 | Progression die, 2 DP
| 8 | Attribute increase, 2 DP
| 10 | Class capstone, 3 DP
| 12+ | Attribute increase every 4 levels, Progression Discipline every 3 levels, 2 DP/level
|===
