= Ancestries & Cultures

Four ancestries inhabit the world of Heroes of Legend.

[%header,cols="2,2,3,3"]
|===
| Ancestry | Dice | Trait | Cultures
| *Human* | +1 Generic | *Versatile:* +1 DP at creation | Imperial, Nomadic, Coastal
| *Elf* | 1 Wind or Water | *Keen Senses:* +1 to perception | High Elf, Wood Elf, Dark Elf
| *Dwarf* | 1 Earth or Fire | *Sturdy:* +2 max HP | Mountain, Deep, Hill
| *Halfling* | 1 Animal | *Lucky:* reroll one 1/session | Riverfolk, Burrower, Wanderer
|===

== Human
Ambitious and adaptable. Humans receive one extra Generic die and one extra DP at character creation. Choose from Imperial (bonus Persuasion), Nomadic (bonus Survival), or Coastal (bonus Athletics) culture.

== Elf
Ancient and mystical. Elves receive either a Wind die (speed, storms, precision) or a Water die (ice, healing, adaptation). *Keen Senses:* +1 bonus to all perception-related rolls. Cultures: High Elf (bonus Arcana), Wood Elf (bonus Stealth), Dark Elf (bonus Deception).

== Dwarf
Sturdy and crafty. Dwarves receive either an Earth die (stability, endurance) or a Fire die (forge-craft, passion). *Sturdy:* +2 maximum HP. Cultures: Mountain (bonus Crafting), Deep (bonus Resilience), Hill (bonus History).

== Halfling
Lucky and nimble. Halflings receive an Animal die. *Lucky:* once per session, reroll one natural 1. Cultures: Riverfolk (bonus Acrobatics), Burrower (bonus Stealth), Wanderer (bonus Streetwise).

== Mixed Ancestry
Optional: half-elves, half-dwarves, etc. Choose one ancestry's dice and the other's trait.
